Rules

All players start with 13 cards, left of dealer starts. Ace high, 2 is higher, 3 is low. Your goal is to get rid of all your cards. The first player places a card, and the next player has to either pass, or place a card of higher value on top and so on until no one can place. The last person who can play a card wins and starts the next round. Winner of the game is "King" and the loser, Scum, has to trade their four most valuable cards with the King.
